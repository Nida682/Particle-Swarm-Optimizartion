2. Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO)

Description:
Particle Swarm Optimization is inspired by the movement of birds or fish. It uses a group of “particles” (solutions) that move through the solution space to find the best answer by following the best-performing particles.

Key idea: Collective intelligence / swarm behavior.

Examples:

1. Neural Network Training
PSO can optimize weights of a neural network instead of using gradient descent.
2. Path Optimization (Robotics/IoT)
Finds the shortest or most efficient path for drones or irrigation systems (related to your smart irrigation project).
